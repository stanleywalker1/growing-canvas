∞ infinite canvas

the first collaborative AI-powered canvas

A space designed for you to collaborate with others on growing an image

think of it as an online graffiti wall - composed by people from around the world. simply describe what you want to see next.

move the box to any corner of the image and describe how you want to expand it

make sure to overlap with the existing image to help keep things blended

think creatively - the more detailed your description the better the results

this experimental project is powered by stable diffusion and hosted with hugging face. it aims to explore the crossroads of AI and creative collaboration. infinitecanvas is still in beta - some features may be inactive.
